Dal Khichdi

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Moong Dal
  • 1 cup of Rice
  • salt to taste
  • Ginger
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Soak dal and rice for about 20 minutes
  2. Choose rice of your choice
  3. In a saucepan create a tampering of chopped ginger
  4. Add curry leaves to saute
  5. Add chopped tomato
  6. Add coriander leaves
  7. Now in another vessel take out the tampering
  8. Add soaked rice and dal to it
  9. Mix it well at medium temperature
  10. Serve hot garnishing it with corainder leaves

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • Dice tomatoes about half a kg
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Butter one and half tbsp

Instructions

  1. Add butter in a pan to low flame
  2. Add cinnamon 1 piece
  3. Add cardamom 2 pieces
  4. Add diced tomatoes and saute them well
  5. Follow the instructions above and take out the pan
  6. Add the mixture to a mixer grinder
  7. Make a puree of it
  8. Take out the puree and strain it using a seive
  9. Now, take out the puree into a khadai
  10. Heat it for about 7 to 8 minutes in low flame
  11. Garnish with breadcrumbs and serve hot

Capsicum Rice

  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  3. Add a little mustard seeds
  4. Wait for it to sputter
  5. Then add Capsicum finely diced along with
  6. Tomato nicely chopped
  7. Wait for it to be cooked in low flame
  8. Add the cooked rice and mix well
  9. Add salt as per taste
  10. Garnish it with Coriander leaves

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200 grams Panner nicely diced in square or rectangle shape
  • Two to Three tomatoes finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter and oil
  • 1 piece small cassia cinnamon bark
  • 2 elaichai
  • Corainder leaves

Instructions

    Part 1

  1. Take a Khadhai
  2. Add 2 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil to it
  3. Add a small piece of cinnamon bark
  4. Add 2 elachai and stir them well in low flame
  5. Add finely chopped tomatoes and stir
  6. Cook the tomatoes until half cooked in low flame

    7. Part 2

  7. Take the mix from part 1
  8. Put it in a mixer grinder to grind
  9. Take out the puree and stain it using a cooking sieve
  10. You are now left with tomato mix
  11. Add finely chopped paneer cubes to the tomato mix
  12. Take the mix in a khadhai and heat it at low flame
  13. Garnish it with corainder leaves and serve hot

Moong Ki Dal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of moong dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Byadagi chillies

Instructions

  1. 1 cup of moong dal in pressure cooker
  2. Rinse it with tap water three times
  3. turmeric to it
  4. Add salt as per taste
  5. Put the cooker on flame and wait for 3 whistles
  6. The dal is cooked
  7. Preheat the byadagi chillies(1 to 2) in a spatula
  8. Break the chillies and add it to cooked dal
  9. Serve hot

Beetroot ki Sabji

Ingredients

  1. 2-3 medium sized beetroots sliced and diced
  2. Salt to taste
  3. Mustard seeds
  4. Red chilli powder
  5. Coriander leaves

Instructions

  • Add cooking oil to Khadai in low flame
  • Add mustard seeds that it sputters
  • Put the diced sliced beetroot into it
  • Add red chilly powder
  • Add salt to taste
  • Heat the mixture for about 15 to 20 minutes in low flame
  • Then take off the flame and serve hot with garnish(coriander leaves)
